Early Life

Future, born Nayvadius DeMun Wilburn on November 20,1983, grew up in Decatur, Georgia a city in DeKalb County in the well known Atlanta metropolitan Area. Wilburn attended Columbia High School in Decatur

Career Beginnings

Future began his career alongside The Dungeon Family, a collective that included his cousin and record producer Rico Wade. Wade encouraged Future to hone his skills as a writer and pursue a rapping career, allowing himself to get away from the street life. Future then signed to A1 Recordings due to his close connection with fellow Atlanta rapper Rocko

Future released a series of mixtapes from 2010 to early 2011.
